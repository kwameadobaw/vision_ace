FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Yes, we accept the National Health Insurance Scheme (NHIS) for eligible services, including routine eye examinations and selected treatments. Please bring your valid NHIS card to your appointment. Some specialist procedures may require private payment or supplementary insurance.

We recommend booking at least 1–2 weeks in advance for routine eye exams. For specialist consultations and surgical procedures, 2–4 weeks' notice is ideal. Emergency or urgent cases can often be seen within 24–48 hours — please call us directly.

Please bring a valid photo ID, your NHIS or insurance card, any current glasses or contact lens prescriptions, a list of medications you are taking, and any previous eye health records if available. Arrive 10–15 minutes early to complete registration forms.

LASIK candidacy depends on several factors including corneal thickness, prescription stability, pupil size, and overall eye health. Book a free LASIK candidacy assessment with us and our refractive surgery team will evaluate whether LASIK or an alternative (LASEK, SMILE) is right for you. Most people over 18 with stable prescriptions are suitable.

We accept cash (GHS), all major debit and credit cards (Visa, Mastercard), mobile money (MTN MoMo, Telecel Cash, AirtelTigo Money), and direct insurance billing for covered services. Payment plans are available for elective surgical procedures — ask our patient coordinators for details.
