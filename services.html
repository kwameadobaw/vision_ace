Our Services

The full spectrum of modern eye care, delivered with expertise and compassion.

Preventive Care

Comprehensive Eye Examinations

A comprehensive eye exam is the cornerstone of good ocular health. Our detailed evaluations go far beyond a simple vision check — we assess the entire health of your eyes.

  • Visual acuity and refraction testing
  • Intraocular pressure measurement (glaucoma screening)
  • Dilated fundus examination
  • Colour vision and contrast sensitivity testing
  • Digital retinal photography
  • Optical coherence tomography (OCT)
Duration: 45–90 min Recommended: Annually

Vision Correction

LASIK & Refractive Surgery

Achieve freedom from glasses and contact lenses with our advanced laser vision correction procedures. Our LASIK platform uses the latest wavefront-guided technology for unparalleled precision.

  • Wavefront-guided LASIK for myopia, hyperopia & astigmatism
  • LASEK / PRK for thin corneas
  • SMILE (Small Incision Lenticule Extraction)
  • Pre-operative candidacy assessment
  • Complete post-operative care protocol
Procedure: ~15 min per eye Recovery: 24–72 hours

Surgical Excellence

Cataract Surgery

Cataracts are the world's leading cause of preventable blindness. Our experienced surgeons perform micro-incision phacoemulsification cataract surgery with a wide selection of premium intraocular lenses (IOLs).

  • Phacoemulsification (micro-incision, sutureless)
  • Monofocal, toric and multifocal IOL options
  • Extended Depth of Focus (EDOF) lenses
  • Femtosecond laser-assisted cataract surgery
  • Comprehensive pre-surgical biometry
Procedure: ~20 min per eye Outpatient: Same-day discharge
Book a Cataract Consultation
Disease Management

Glaucoma Management

Glaucoma is often called the "silent thief of sight" because it causes irreversible damage before symptoms appear. Our comprehensive glaucoma service ensures early detection and effective long-term management.

  • Visual field testing (perimetry)
  • OCT optic nerve analysis
  • Gonioscopy and pachymetry
  • Topical medication management
  • Selective Laser Trabeculoplasty (SLT)
  • Trabeculectomy and tube shunt surgery
Follow-up: Every 3–6 months Goal: Preserve your vision

Children's Eye Care

Pediatric Eye Care

Children's eyes change rapidly during development. Early detection and treatment of vision problems is critical to academic success and quality of life. Our pediatric team makes every child's visit comfortable and even fun.

  • Newborn and infant eye screening
  • Amblyopia (lazy eye) detection and treatment
  • Strabismus (squint) management and surgery
  • Myopia progression control (orthokeratology, atropine)
  • School vision assessments
  • Congenital eye condition management
Ages: Newborn to 18 First exam: Age 6 months
Book a Pediatric Exam
Subspecialty Care

Retinal Services

Our retinal specialists are trained to diagnose and treat a full range of retinal diseases, from diabetic retinopathy to age-related macular degeneration and retinal detachment.

  • Diabetic retinopathy screening and laser treatment
  • Age-related macular degeneration (AMD) management
  • Anti-VEGF intravitreal injections
  • Retinal detachment surgery (vitrectomy)
  • Fluorescein angiography
  • Epiretinal membrane treatment
Technology: Wide-field OCT Diabetics: Annual screening

Contact Lens Services

Whether you're a first-time wearer or need a specialty lens fit, our contact lens specialists provide expert fitting and a wide selection of premium lens options.

Advanced Procedures

Corneal Services

The cornea is central to good vision. Our cornea specialists manage everything from chronic dry eye to keratoconus and corneal transplantation.

  • Keratoconus diagnosis and management
  • Corneal collagen cross-linking (CXL)
  • Penetrating and deep anterior lamellar keratoplasty
  • DSEK/DMEK for Fuchs' dystrophy
  • Advanced dry eye disease management
  • Corneal ulcer and infection treatment
