About Vision Ace

Two decades of compassionate, expert eye care rooted in trust and innovation.

Our Mission

Seeing Clearly, Living Fully

Vision Ace Eye Associates was founded with a single, unwavering purpose: to preserve and enhance the precious gift of sight for every patient who walks through our doors. We believe that exceptional eye care is not a luxury — it is a fundamental necessity for a fulfilling life.

Our clinic brings together the finest ophthalmologists, optometrists, and clinical support staff, united by a shared commitment to clinical excellence, patient dignity, and continuous innovation.

Our Journey

A Story Built on Trust & Vision

2004

The Beginning

Dr. Esi Mensah founded Vision Ace Eye Associates in Accra with a small team of two, driven by a vision to bring world-class ophthalmology to West Africa.

2008

Expanding Our Reach

Opened our second location and introduced LASIK refractive surgery services, becoming one of the first clinics in the region to offer wavefront-guided laser correction.

2013

Technology Investment

Installed state-of-the-art OCT imaging and introduced a dedicated pediatric eye care unit staffed by fellowship-trained pediatric ophthalmologists.

2018

Regional Recognition

Awarded "Best Eye Care Provider" by the Ghana Health Awards. Surpassed 10,000 successful cataract surgeries and launched our community outreach free screening program.

2024

A New Chapter

Celebrating 20 years, we opened our flagship state-of-the-art facility featuring a dedicated surgical suite, digital retinal imaging center, and a cornea specialty clinic.
