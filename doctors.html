Fellowship-trained, compassionate, and dedicated to your visual health.

Our Team

Expert Care, Human Touch

Our team of eight specialists brings together over 80 years of combined clinical experience.

EM

Dr. Esi Mensah

Founder & Medical Director Comprehensive Ophthalmology · LASIK · Cataract

Dr. Mensah founded Vision Ace in 2004 after completing her fellowship in refractive surgery at Moorfields Eye Hospital, London. With over 8,000 successful LASIK procedures, she is one of West Africa's most experienced refractive surgeons.

MBChB, FWACS Fellowship – Moorfields London
Book with Dr. Mensah
KO

Dr. Kweku Osei

Consultant Ophthalmologist Glaucoma · Optic Nerve Disease

A specialist in glaucoma and optic nerve disease, Dr. Osei trained at the University of Ghana Medical School and completed a glaucoma fellowship at the University of Michigan's Kellogg Eye Center.

MBChB, FWACS, MRCOphth Fellowship – Kellogg Eye Center, Michigan
Book with Dr. Osei
AA

Dr. Abena Asante

Pediatric Ophthalmologist Pediatric Eye Care · Strabismus · Amblyopia

Dr. Asante is our beloved children's eye specialist. She completed her pediatric ophthalmology fellowship at Great Ormond Street Hospital, London, and has a special interest in strabismus surgery and myopia control in children.

MBChB, FWACS Fellowship – Great Ormond Street, London
Book with Dr. Asante
KB

Dr. Kofi Boateng

Vitreoretinal Surgeon Retina · Vitreous · Diabetic Eye Disease

Dr. Boateng is our vitreoretinal specialist, trained at Johns Hopkins Wilmer Eye Institute. He performs complex retinal detachment repairs, manages diabetic retinopathy, and has pioneered the use of anti-VEGF therapy at our clinic.

MBChB, PhD, FWACS Fellowship – Wilmer Eye Institute, Johns Hopkins
Book with Dr. Boateng
EA

Dr. Efua Agyeman

Cornea & External Disease Specialist Cornea · Dry Eye · Keratoconus · Transplants

Dr. Agyeman specialises in diseases of the cornea and external eye. She completed her cornea fellowship at the Massachusetts Eye and Ear Infirmary, Harvard Medical School, and performs all forms of corneal transplantation.

MBChB, FWACS, MSc Fellowship – Mass Eye & Ear, Harvard
Book with Dr. Agyeman
JA

Dr. James Appiah

Consultant Optometrist Contact Lenses · Low Vision · Neuro-Optometry

Dr. Appiah leads our optometry department and is our contact lens expert. With a Doctor of Optometry degree from the University of Waterloo, he specialises in specialty contact lens fitting and low vision rehabilitation.

OD (Waterloo), FAAO Specialty: Scleral & Ortho-K Lenses
Book with Dr. Appiah
Clinical Team

The Team Behind the Team

Every outstanding clinical outcome is supported by a dedicated, highly trained clinical support team.

🏥

Ophthalmic Nurses

Our certified ophthalmic nurses ensure seamless patient flow, pre- and post-operative care, and a warm, reassuring presence throughout your visit.

🔧

Ophthalmic Technicians

Skilled in operating and maintaining our diagnostic equipment — from OCT machines to visual field analyzers — ensuring every test is performed with precision.

💬

Patient Care Coordinators

Our coordinators handle everything from appointment scheduling and insurance queries to guiding you through surgical planning and follow-up care.

🔬

Orthoptists

Specialists in ocular motility and binocular vision, our orthoptists work closely with our pediatric team on squint assessments and non-surgical management.
